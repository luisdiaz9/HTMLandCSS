Max Temperature

The scatter graph shows the number of cities with a variety of temperatures in percentage per degree of Latitude on June 19th 2019. The number of cities with high temperature skyrocketed from -40 to zero degrees of latitude. On the other hand, the number of cities with low temperatures decreased from 20 to 80 degrees of latitude. It is worth to mention that an order 2 polynomial trendline, with a negative coefficient of the x2 , was observed.
